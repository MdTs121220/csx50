About Me

I am a State Vocational Teacher who teaches in a school far away from the crowds. My current assignment is SMK Negeri 3 Sorong in West Papua, precisely in Sorong City. I teach in the Electricity department because I am a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering graduate. I have devoted myself as a SMK teacher since 2006. I am currently a Civil Servant with the Functional Position of Teacher. I am also a father with 3 daughters. My wife is also a teacher who teaches Chemistry in one of the public high schools in Sorong City. I am a teacher who likes to give something new and useful for my students. I like my students to develop towards the quality of their competence. I also like to learn interesting and useful things to give to my students. My students are those who I consider my children too. They are the future leaders of the nation who will continue the struggle of the Indonesian nation. I must educate them with full struggle for their better future and useful for the nation and state. I also like listening to music, I often listen to all kinds of music while working or studying.
